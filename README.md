## About

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Energy and Petroleum Engineering at the
University of Wyoming. My research asks where, and how, we can safely store
hydrogen underground at the scale a clean-energy economy will demand — studying
engineered caverns in the bedded salt and trona of Wyoming's Green River Basin,
along with depleted reservoirs and aquifers, through the combined lens of
reservoir engineering, geomechanics, and numerical simulation.

## Research interests

- Underground hydrogen storage (salt & trona caverns, depleted reservoirs, aquifers)
- Reservoir geomechanics and cavern stability
- Reservoir engineering and numerical simulation
- Energy transition and net-zero pathways
